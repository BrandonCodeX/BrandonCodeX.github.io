Trip to Kenya



Day 1, 2, 3 - Nairobi

Meet and Greet and transfer to Hemingways Nairobi. After a leisurely breakfast and briefing, spend the day touring the Giraffe Center, Kazuri Bead Factory, and enjoying a behind-the-scenes tour at the Daphne Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage.

You’ll also have the option to explore the Karen Blixen Home. That evening there will be a welcome cocktail and dinner.






Day 4, 5, 6 - Tsavo West National Park

On day 3 after breakfast, transfer to airport to board a bush plane to Tsavo National Park. Upon arrival, we will travel via game drive to Finch Hattons and begin our safari experience. You will experience traditional vehicle-based wildlife drives over the next few days with many opportunities to encounter thrilling wildlife, bird watch, relax at the lodge, enjoy the pool or spa, do some yoga classes, go for a hike, learn all about the amazing ecosystem and interact with some of the local peoples.







Day 6, 7, 8, 9 - Masa Mara national reserve

Take a scenic bush flight from Tsavo National Park to the Masai Mara. Upon arrival, we will travel via a game drive to Sanctuary Olonana. Here you’ll experience the sweeping vistas of the legendary Masai Mara and have numerous opportunities to encounter the “Big 5". This is also where, if you are here at the right time, you will potentially witness migration. You’ll also interact with the Maasai people and have the opportunity to go on a balloon ride.





Day 9, 10 - Nairobi

After breakfast at Sanctuary Olonana, you will have one final safari excursion and then you will head to the airstrip for a bush flight to Nairobi where we will enjoy a final lunch together at Carnivore Restaurant and you’ll be able to either rest in a day room or stay the night before departing Africa or continue on with an optional excursion.
